Tornado

Tornado Watch:

  • Conditions are right for development of tornadoes.
  • A tornado watch does not mean that a tornado has been sighted.
  • Notification: Tornado watch will be announced through local broadcast media.

Tornado Warning:

  • Indicates that a tornado has been sighted by radar or bona fide emergency agency.
  • Notification: Tornado warning will be announced through media and of the following:
    • A three to five minute sounding of the City of Wheaton tornado sirens.
    • Through cell phone contact.
